What We Do

Our Services

⬜ ⬛

Stone Work

Custom stonework for walls, facades, fireplaces, and feature walls. We source and lay natural and manufactured stone to bring lasting beauty to any project.

🧱

Brick Work

Professional bricklaying for new builds and additions — exterior walls, chimneys, archways, and decorative features built to Ontario's highest standards.

🏗️

Foundation Repair

Structural crack repair, waterproofing, and foundation reinforcement. We assess and restore foundations to ensure your home stays safe and dry for decades.

🪨

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining walls using natural stone, concrete block, or brick — ideal for sloped landscapes, garden terracing, and property boundary solutions.

🏛️

Restoration

Breathing new life into existing structures — repointing, tuckpointing, and full restoration of walls, fences, and historic brickwork to their original strength and appearance.

🏡

Patios, Stairs & Flooring

Elegant outdoor living spaces crafted in natural flagstone, flex stone, and pavers — patios, walkways, staircases, and stone flooring designed to impress and endure.
